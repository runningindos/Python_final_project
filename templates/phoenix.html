Buddy's Top 3 Parks in Dublin City!

First Place: Phoenix Park

Phoenix Park is a large urban park located in the heart of Dublin, Ireland. It is one of the largest enclosed recreational spaces within any European capital city and covers an impressive 1,750 acres (707 hectares). The park is located about 2 to 4 kilometers west of the city center, making it easily accessible for both locals and tourists.

Visitors to Phoenix Park can enjoy a mix of natural beauty, historical significance, and recreational opportunities, making it a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists in Dublin.
